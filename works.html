Works

What the Larp considers most important to fans who look forward to our works.
Those fans who cry, laugh, and have their larping hearts touched by the stories we bring to life
Thinking of each and every larper's face on the other side of the screen, we wish to create larp that makes them say "I'm glad I larped", that makes them want to watch it over and over again
As a guild, we strive to deliver larp that will remain in the hearts and minds of our fellow larpers, while changing new forms of expression and experimenting with various genres without focusing on any particular style
